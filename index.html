Privacy Policy - RentMate

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to RentMate, a smart rental management and roommate coordination platform. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What information we collect.
  • How we use and protect your data.
  • Your rights regarding your personal information.
  • How you can contact us.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or use RentMate, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (for OTP verification)
  • Profile details (gender, preferences, bio)
  • Profile photo (optional)

1.2 Property & Rental Information

  • Property details (address, rent amount, deposit)
  • Lease start and end dates
  • Roommate details
  • Payment and expense records

1.3 Usage Data

  • Device information and IP address
  • Login timestamps
  • App interaction data
  • Error logs and diagnostics

1.4 Payment Information

Rent payments and transactions are processed securely through third-party payment gateways. We do not store your debit/credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Enable rent tracking and expense splitting.
  • Send rent reminders and notifications.
  • Facilitate roommate communication.
  • Process secure payments.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Secure authentication and encrypted connections (HTTPS).
  • Role-based access control.
  • Regular system monitoring and updates.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your profile information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Opt out of promotional communications.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Authentication providers (e.g., OTP services)
  • Payment gateways
  • Cloud hosting services

These providers are obligated to protect your data and use it only for intended purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

RentMate is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted within the app and on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@rentmate.com
  • App: Use the "Help & Support" section inside the app.
